Enhance the team member system with unique invite links (build on what was implemented yesterday).

Current state: Team leaders can mark themselves as "Team Leader" and invite team members. Team members share the team branding.

New requirements:

1. Unique Invite Links for Team Members
   - When a team leader invites or adds a team member, generate a unique handle for that agent (e.g., first name, or firstname-lastname if needed).
   - Create unique invite links in this format:
     - Team main page: maintainhome.ai/[teamhandle]   (e.g. maintainhome.ai/diamondgroup)
     - Individual team member link: maintainhome.ai/[teamhandle]/[agenthandle]   (e.g. maintainhome.ai/diamondgroup/john or maintainhome.ai/diamondgroup/sarah)
   - When a client visits maintainhome.ai/[teamhandle]/[agenthandle], show the full team branding (logo, colors, tagline, welcome message) but remember which specific agent invited them.

2. Team Member Profile
   - Team members can upload their own headshot and phone number (but NOT their own logo or change colors — those stay with the team leader).
   - These appear on their personal invite page and in the "Your Home Support" widget for their clients.

3. Broker Dashboard Updates
   - Team Leader Dashboard: Show clients grouped by team member (e.g. "John's Clients", "Sarah's Clients"). Include a section showing all team members with their unique invite links (copy button for each).
   - Team Member Dashboard: When a team member logs in, they see only their own clients, using the shared team branding. They also get a "My Invite Link" button that copies maintainhome.ai/[teamhandle]/[theirhandle].

4. Client Assignment
   - When a client signs up through any team member's unique link, they are automatically assigned to that specific agent.
   - Team leader can still see all clients and re-assign if needed.

5. Technical Notes
   - Store agent_handle separately for team members.
   - Use path-based routing: /:teamHandle/:agentHandle? 
   - Keep branding pulled from the team leader's white_label_configs record.
   - Make sure the system falls back gracefully if someone visits just the team handle.
